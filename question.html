Questions

What are the principles of analysis and design?

A list of methods which can be followed to design a website that are both good looking and easy to use.

  • 1. Contrast.
  • 2. Balance.
  • 3. Emphasis.
  • 4. Proportion.
  • 5. Hierarchy.
  • 6. Repetition.
  • 7. Rhythm.
  • 8. Pattern.
  • 9. White space.
  • 10. Movement.
  • 11. Variety.
  • 12. Unity.
What are website architectural requirements?

Website architectural is the method to structure a website to ensure that it meets the business goal and deliver a great experience for the users.

  • 1. Frame your approach.
  • 2. Understand the business goals.
  • 3. Understand the user.
  • 4. Create personas.
  • 5. Gather your content.
  • 6. Run a card sort.
What are website design structures, including hierarchy and navigation design?

Website structures refers to the hierarchy, order and organisation of website pages, mapped out by a desired user experience. It connects all the web pages with a navigational system of menus, internal linking, and content.

What are user-interface design requirements and production processes?

Interface design process involves creating wireframes, mock-ups, and prototype of the visual elements and interactions which should take account of the user flows, and usability testing to ensure great experience for the user.

What are programming controls and design structures?

The essential components that dictates the flow of execution within a program.

What are website testing procedures?

Testing end-user scenarios on a website to test its behaviour.

  • 1. Ensure cross browser compatibility.
  • 2. Test for responsiveness.
  • 3. Functionality testing.
  • 4. Check for broken links.
  • 5. Ensure security.
  • 6. Test payment gateways.
  • 7. Cookie testing.
